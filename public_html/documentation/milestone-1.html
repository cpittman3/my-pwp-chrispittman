PWP Milestone 1

Purpose

This project will be a portfolio type site for the purpose of attracting potential clients.
The main service to be provided is 3D art and specialized low-poly models for games and software.

Audience

This site will accommodate a wide range of users who have need of original art or models.
Including, but not limited to:
Game developers who need outside help in prop creation to meet a strict deadline.
Packaging designers who need original and product specific art.
Web developers looking for specific original art for their websites.
3d storyboard creators needing props and sets.
Original cover art for beginning authors and bands.
Models built to strict specifications for demonstrating mechanical parts or prototypes.
Custom content for 3D software such as Poser, Daz3d, Moviestorm, and iClone.
Custom content for game engines such as Unreal Engine, Unity, and Godot.

Goal

The main goal of this site is for users to be able to view my work and be able to contact me and contract my services.

Persona

Mark Yang, Age 27

Mark owns a 16-inch M1 MacBook Pro running Big Sur OS,
iPhone 12 iOS 14 A14 Bionic chip, and
an iPad Air with Apple iOS 7 and an A7 Processor.

Mark is fairly tech-savvy, prefers Apple devices and could be described as a power user.

Mark is an author who prefers to create his own cover art. He is proficient with Poser Pro and has
a good understanding of advanced lighting such as IBL and ray tracing techniques. Most models he
needs are available through Poser's content store, however, he also needs custom models that are
unique to his books such as characters and creatures.

As a power user I expect to get a quick overview of the artist's capabilities and availability without sorting through clutter

Mark prefers High quality high-poly models. He needs them to be rigged in a method that is compatible with
Poser Pro. He needs a full range of texture maps that meet industry standards and take advantage of
modern lighting techniques. He needs for the models to be presented in multiple formats that are compatible
with Poser Pro and retain necessary data.

Mark does not want an artist who does not maintain regular contact and visual updates on the model.
The Artist must be able to complete the models in a reasonable amount of time.
He does not want an artist unwilling to create the model based on his specifications.

Mark's main goal is to assess my abilities and to be able to contact me if he wants to hire me.

Additional information Mark would need to know would be my current availability.
